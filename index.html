Who We Are

We exist to share the love of Jesus, serve our local community, and grow together in faith.

Our Vision

To be a global voice of hope and a center of Christian excellence, raising leaders who influence their generation for Christ.

Our Mission

Empowering believers through the Word, Worship, and Works of service to impact the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Core Values

We uphold Integrity, Compassion, Excellence, and Faith in God as the pillars of our ministry and daily lives.
